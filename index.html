Our Story

Traditional Values, Modern Dining

Founded in 1985, GV Tiffins brings the authentic taste of South India to your table. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the traditional flavors while embracing modern, health-conscious cooking techniques.

We source the finest ingredients and prepare everything fresh daily, just like it would be in a traditional South Indian home.
