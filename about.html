About GV Tiffins

Our story, values, and commitment to authentic South Indian cuisine

Our Story

GV Tiffins was founded in 1985 by Mr. G. Venkataraman, who had a vision to bring the authentic taste of South India to Bangalore. What started as a small eatery with just four tables has now grown into a beloved restaurant cherished by locals and tourists alike.

Our founder's commitment to traditional recipes, quality ingredients, and a welcoming atmosphere has been the cornerstone of our success for over three decades. Today, the restaurant is managed by the second generation of the family, who continue to honor these traditions while introducing subtle innovations.

The name "Guruvayoor Tiffins" (now known simply as "GV Tiffins") was inspired by the famous Guruvayoor Temple in Kerala, reflecting our roots in South Indian tradition and spirituality.

What We Stand For

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Authenticity

We stay true to traditional South Indian recipes and cooking methods, preserving the authentic flavors and techniques passed down through generations.

Quality

We use only the freshest, highest quality ingredients, sourced from trusted local suppliers to ensure the best taste and nutritional value.

Family

We treat our customers as extended family, creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home and well-cared for.

Health-Conscious

We prioritize healthy cooking methods, balancing traditional techniques with modern health awareness to create nutritious, wholesome meals.

Community

We actively engage with and support our local community, recognizing our responsibility to give back to the people who have supported us.

Sustainability

We implement eco-friendly practices, reduce waste, and minimize our environmental footprint in all aspects of our operations.
